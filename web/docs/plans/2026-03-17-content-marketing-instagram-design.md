## 2. Instagram Content Strategy

### Schedule: 3 posts/week — Tuesday, Thursday, Saturday

### 6 Post Types (rotating)

1. **Price Showdown** (1x/week) — Compare a product across 3-4 suppliers. Table format, green highlight on lowest price.
2. **Price Drop Alert** (1x/week) — Urgency-driven, red accents, before/after price, link to site.
3. **Weekly Top 5** (2x/month) — Ranked list of cheapest products in a category.
4. **Dato Dental** (2x/month) — Educational tip with product tie-in. Expert positioning.
5. **Proveedor vs Proveedor** (2x/month) — Head-to-head supplier comparison. Spicy, engagement-driving.
6. **Meme / Hot Take** (2x/month) — Dental humor with challenger tone. Relatable, shareable.

### Hashtag Strategy

Rotate between 3 sets, always include `#DentalPrecios` + 2 from each set (6-8 total):

- **Set A (discovery):** `#InsumoDental #OdontologiaChile #DentistaChile #ProductosDentales #InsumosDentalesChile`
- **Set B (niche):** `#CompositeResina #Resina3M #MaterialDental #OdontologiaCL #ConsultorioDental`
- **Set C (price/value):** `#PreciosDentales #AhorrosDentales #ComparadorDental #DentalPrecios #MejorPrecio`

### Location: `/blog` on dentalprecios.cl
### Frequency: 2 posts/month
### Word count: 1,500-2,500 words per post

### 4 Content Pillars

1. **Guia de compra** — "Mejores resinas dentales 2026: Comparativa de precios en Chile"
2. **Comparativa de proveedores** — "Techdent vs MayorDent: Donde comprar insumos mas baratos?"

```
H1: [Title with primary keyword]
```

## 5. Content Guidelines — "Not So AI"

To keep content feeling human and edgy:
- Use Chilean slang sparingly but naturally ("wena", "la firme")
- Imperfect punctuation in casual posts (no periods at end of captions)
- Opinions and hot takes, not neutral descriptions
- Reference real dental frustrations (supplier price hikes, stock issues)
- Memes use current formats, not generic templates
- Numbers always specific, never rounded ("$12.490" not "aprox $12.000")
- Captions vary in length — some are 2 lines, some are a paragraph
