# DentalPrecios.cl — SEO Audit & Keyword Strategy

**Date:** March 2026
**Domain:** dentalprecios.cl
**Category:** B2B dental supply price comparison — Chile

---

## Executive Summary

DentalPrecios.cl occupies a category with **zero direct competitors** in Chile. No one is aggregating dental supply prices across vendors into a single comparison tool. The existing players (Techdent, MayorDent, Dentobal, DentalStore, Depodental, ExproDental) are all individual e-commerce stores competing against each other — none offer cross-vendor comparison. This is a massive first-mover advantage.

The top 3 priorities are: (1) capture high-intent product search traffic with SEO-optimized comparison pages and blog content, (2) build topical authority in "insumos dentales Chile" through a content cluster strategy, and (3) implement technical SEO foundations (structured data, sitemap, meta tags) to ensure every product page is indexed and ranked.

**Overall assessment:** Strong product with an open playing field. The site needs a content + SEO layer to convert that product advantage into organic traffic.

## Content Gap — What No One Is Creating

1. **Product comparison content** — "Filtek Z350 vs Tetric N-Ceram: precio, propiedades y cuál elegir" — nobody in Chile writes this
2. **Price guides** — "¿Cuánto cuesta el composite dental en Chile? Guía de precios 2026" — huge search intent, zero good content
3. **Category guides** — "Mejores adhesivos dentales disponibles en Chile: comparación completa"
4. **Vendor directories** — "Todas las tiendas de insumos dentales en Chile: comparación de envío, precios y stock"
5. **Educational + commercial hybrid** — "Cómo elegir el composite correcto para cada caso clínico (y dónde comprarlo más barato)"

| Meta descriptions | Build | Compelling descriptions with price + CTA |
| H1 tags | Verify | One per page, includes primary keyword |
| Structured data | Add | Product schema (name, price, availability, vendor) |
| Open Graph tags | Add | For social sharing of comparisons |
| Page speed | Optimize | Compress images, lazy load product grids |
| Mobile UX | Verify | Price comparison tables must work on mobile |

| robots.txt | Verify | Not blocking product pages |
| HTTPS | Verify | All pages served over SSL |
