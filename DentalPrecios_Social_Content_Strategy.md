# DentalPrecios — Social Content Strategy
## Instagram & TikTok | Q2–Q3 2026

---

## The Thesis

DentalPrecios is not a store. It's not a catalog. It's the only platform in Latin America where a clinician can search a dental product and get real prices across vendors, technical specs, clinical tips, and educational content — in one place.

No one else is doing this. Not in Chile. Not in LatAm.

The social strategy exists to make that impossible to ignore.

---

## Positioning: The Angle

**We are not a dental supply shop with an Instagram account.** Every vendor already does that — mediocre product photos, generic "available now" captions, zero clinical value.

DentalPrecios is the platform that treats clinicians like the professionals they are: giving them the information they actually need to make better purchasing and clinical decisions — pricing transparency, product intelligence, and clinical context — for free.

**The social voice should feel like:** a sharp colleague who knows the market cold, has strong opinions backed by data, and isn't afraid to say what every dentist is thinking but no one is posting.

---

## Audience

**Primary:** Dentists in private practice across Latin America (25–45), digitally active, purchasing their own supplies, hungry for clinical and commercial information they can't easily find elsewhere.

**Secondary:** Dental students and recent graduates entering the market, building their material preferences, price-sensitive, high social media consumption.

**Psychographic profile:** Frustrated by opaque pricing. Tired of guessing which product is best. Values efficiency. Respects expertise. Shares content that makes them look informed.

## Content Pillars

### Pillar 1 — "The Real Price" (Provocative / Disruptive)
Expose the pricing reality of the dental supply market. Bold, confrontational, designed to stop the scroll.

**The energy:** "Nobody wants to say this, so we will."

**Post types:**
- Price reveals: "Un composite puede costar $20.000 o $55.000 en Chile. Misma jeringa. Diferente tienda. ¿Sabías?"
- Vendor comparison callouts: "Buscamos el mismo adhesivo en 15 tiendas. La diferencia de precio: 47%."
- Market myths: "El material más caro NO es el mejor material. Te mostramos por qué."
- "Cuánto cuesta realmente..." series: Break down the real cost of common procedures by supply input

**Format:** Bold text overlays on dark backgrounds. Data-driven. Numbers front and center. Carousel breakdowns on IG, fast-cut narrated reveals on TikTok.

**Frequency:** 2x/week

---

### Pillar 2 — "Product Intelligence" (Clinical Authority)
Showcase what makes DentalPrecios unique: every product page is a clinical resource — not just a listing. Specs, indications, handling tips, head-to-head comparisons.

**The energy:** "The information you wish the manufacturer gave you."

**Post types:**
- Product deep dives: "Filtek Z350 XT — lo que dice la ficha técnica vs. lo que pasa en boca"
- Head-to-head comparisons: "Tetric N-Ceram vs Z350: precio, manejo, pulido, indicaciones. Todo en una página."
- "Clinical tip in 30 seconds" — quick handling/application tip tied to a specific product
- "Lo que no te dice el catálogo" — reveal product nuances that only show up in clinical use
- Feature spotlights: show how a DentalPrecios product page looks vs. a typical online store listing

**Format:** Split-screen comparisons, annotated product page screenshots, short talking-head tips on TikTok, carousel infographics on IG.

**Frequency:** 2x/week

---

### Pillar 3 — "El Blog Que Nadie Más Escribe" (Content Authority / Traffic Driver)
Position the DentalPrecios blog as the go-to clinical + commercial resource that no vendor, no distributor, and no other platform is creating.

**The energy:** "We wrote the article you've been googling for."

**Post types:**
- Blog teasers with a killer hook: "¿Cuánto cuesta realmente el composite en Chile? Escribimos la guía que no existía. Link en bio."
- Data snippets from blog posts turned into carousel slides
- "Pregunta que nadie responde" — take a common clinician question, answer it in 60 seconds, then point to the full blog post
- Behind-the-content: "Analizamos los precios de 7.800+ productos para escribir este artículo. Esto es lo que encontramos."
- Quote cards from blog posts — sharp, quotable insights formatted as standalone posts

**Format:** Text-heavy carousels on IG (3–5 slides, clean typography), 30–60 second narrated summaries on TikTok, story sequences with swipe-up/link sticker.

**Frequency:** 1–2x/week (tied to blog publishing cadence)

---

### Pillar 4 — "Hot Takes & Debate" (Engagement Engine)
Stir conversation. Force opinions. Build community through controlled controversy.

**The energy:** "We have an opinion. What's yours?"

**Post types:**
- Polls and "this or that": "¿Z350 o Tetric? Vota y explica."
- Unpopular opinions: "Opinión impopular: el composite más barato de tu tienda favorita probablemente es suficiente para el 70% de tus restauraciones posteriores."
- "Defiende tu material": invite followers to argue for their preferred product
- Myth-busting: "3 cosas que los dentistas creen sobre adhesivos que no son ciertas"
- Reaction content: react to common dental supply marketing claims

**Format:** Poll stickers in stories, text-overlay reels, duet/stitch format on TikTok, comment-bait carousels on IG.

**Frequency:** 1–2x/week

## Platform-Specific Guidelines

### Instagram
- **Feed:** Carousels and static graphics dominate. Clean, dark aesthetic (navy/white/accent from brand kit). Typography-forward. Minimal product photography — this isn't a catalog.
- **Reels:** 15–45 seconds. Hook in first 2 seconds. Text overlays always (most watch muted). End with CTA or provocative question.
- **Stories:** Blog links, polls, quick behind-the-scenes of data analysis, "did you know" facts.
- **Bio link:** Always point to dentalprecios.cl with UTM tracking. Rotate featured link (blog post of the week, comparison tool, trending product).

### TikTok
- **Style:** Fast, direct, slightly raw. Not overproduced. The voice should feel like a smart colleague talking to you between patients.
- **Hook formula:** Open with a bold claim or surprising number. "¿Sabías que el mismo composite puede costar 47% más dependiendo de dónde lo compres?"
- **Duets & Stitches:** React to dental influencer content, vendor marketing claims, or common clinical questions.
- **Trending sounds:** Use selectively — only when they genuinely amplify the message. Never force it.
- **Length:** 15–60 seconds. Prefer 30.

## Voice & Tone Guide

### We sound like:
- A colleague who did the research you didn't have time to do
- Direct, sharp, confident — not arrogant
- Data-driven but never dry
- Clinically precise when it matters, conversational when it doesn't
- Bilingual-comfortable: primarily Spanish, but technical terms in English are fine (composite, bulk fill, bonding agent)

### We never sound like:
- A dental supply store posting product photos
- A brand ambassador pushing a single manufacturer
- An academic lecture
- Desperate for engagement ("LIKE si estás de acuerdo!!!")

### Tone spectrum by pillar:

| Pillar | Tone |
|--------|------|
| The Real Price | Bold, provocative, almost confrontational |
| Product Intelligence | Authoritative, precise, clinical |
| Blog teasers | Curious, inviting, "you need to read this" |
| Hot Takes & Debate | Playful, opinionated, community-oriented |

## Key Metrics & KPIs

### Growth (Monthly targets — first 90 days):
- IG followers: +500/month
- TikTok followers: +1,000/month
- Profile visits to dentalprecios.cl (from social): +300/month

### Engagement:
- IG engagement rate: target 4–6% (niche professional accounts typically outperform consumer benchmarks)
- TikTok average views per video: target 2x follower count
- Save rate on carousels: target 3%+ (indicates clinical utility)
- Comment rate on Hot Takes posts: target 2%+

### Content performance signals:
- **Saves** = the post delivered clinical or commercial value
- **Shares** = the post said something the audience wanted to broadcast
- **Comments** = the post provoked a reaction or opinion
- **Link clicks** = the post drove traffic to the platform

Prioritize saves and shares over likes. Likes are vanity. Saves and shares indicate content that actually matters to clinicians.
